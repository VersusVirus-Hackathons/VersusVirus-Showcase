#LOKALEREINKAUF

e-commerce marketplace for local shops

Wordpress, Woocomerce, PHP, HTML, JavaScript, CSS, Powerpoint, Windows Moviemaker

It is challenging to try to explain in a few words the whole project.

We know that this platform will help small businesses to market their own products, so they don't need to deal with technical stuff and they can concentrate only on their customers.

When the same people are inspired by an idea that will help somebody, great things are happing.

- Recruit the shops that want to sell through our platform (small shops, local producers, farmers) - Fine tune payment flows & bug fixing - Go Live - Promote the platform and create more visibility through social media
