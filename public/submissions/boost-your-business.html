Description

During the crisis the companies have the need to optimize their businesses. What they learn, they can keep for the future. The big thing is to get to know, what the possibilites and chances are and how to implement them. Other companies and suppliers can help to find the right solution. To make this work, we simplified to the wordpress-post for the company and the supplier. We included a filter for the target group and a review system. The supplier side is highly interested to show their solutions and the company side has a need to find solutions and feedback from implementig in no time.

Technologies used

Wordpress with plugins and some php.

Accomplishments

We could cover everything from concept over marketing and sales to customer success and technology.

Obstacles

Organising the team related to tasks and skills.

Learnings

To focus on simplicity and think big.

Next steps

Testing the prototype with real stores and suppliers and jumping into deatails.
